About us

"Helping-Hands Trust (HHT)" is an Indian non-profit and non-governmental organization that provides care and protection to street and working children in India. HHT provides residential care, education and skill-building programs, healthcare support, and talent development activities. The overall health and hygiene of children needs to be given more attention to strengthen their immunity and protect them from life-threatening diseases. Your generous donation can keep them safe and ensure they receive healthcare facilities during these challenging times.
